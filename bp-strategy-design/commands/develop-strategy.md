# /develop-strategy -- Strategy Development

Create a complete business strategy using integrated frameworks.

## Invocation

```
/develop-strategy Our healthcare technology division
/develop-strategy E-commerce business in Latin America
/develop-strategy [business unit or company]
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand Strategic Context

Gather essential context:
- **Current state**: Performance, position, capabilities
- **Strategic challenges**: What problems need solving?
- **Ambition**: What does success look like in 3-5 years?
- **Constraints**: Budget, timeline, organizational, regulatory

### Step 2: Apply Strategic Frameworks

**1. External Analysis**
- PEST Analysis (`pest-analysis`)
- Porter's Five Forces (`porters-five-forces`)
- Market Definition (`market-definition`)
- Competitor Analysis (`competitor-analysis`)

**2. Internal Analysis**
- SWOT Analysis (`swot-analysis`)
- Value Chain (`value-chain`)
- Capability Assessment (`capability-assessment`)

**3. Strategic Positioning**
- Value Disciplines (`value-disciplines`)
- Customer Segmentation (`customer-segmentation`)
- Competitive Advantage (`competitive-advantage`)

**4. Strategic Options**
- Scenario Development (`scenario-development`)
- Portfolio Matrix (`portfolio-matrix`)
- Prioritization (`prioritization-funnel`)

### Step 4: Offer Next Steps

- "Want me to **articulate this strategy** for different audiences?"
- "Should I **develop scenarios** for key uncertainties?"
- "Want me to **create a communication plan** for rollout?"
- "Should I **build a financial model** for this strategy?"

## Notes

- Strategy is about choices — be explicit about what you're NOT doing
- Test strategy against scenarios to ensure robustness
- Balance aspiration with achievability
- Make it actionable with clear initiatives and owners
- Review and adapt regularly
