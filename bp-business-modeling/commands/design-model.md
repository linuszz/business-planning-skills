# /design-model -- Business Model Design

Create comprehensive business model using proven frameworks.

## Invocation

```
/design-model Subscription box service for pet owners
/design-model B2B SaaS marketplace platform
/design-model [business description]
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand Business Context

Gather essential information:
- Value proposition
- Target customers
- Revenue model
- Key resources needed
- Competitive landscape

### Step 2: Apply Business Model Frameworks

**1. Business Definition** (`business-definition`)
- Define scope and boundaries
- Clarify value creation

**2. SWOT Analysis** (`swot-analysis`)
- Assess internal capabilities
- Identify external factors

**3. Value Chain** (`value-chain`)
- Map key activities
- Identify differentiators

**4. RACI Analysis** (`raci-analysis`)
- Define roles and responsibilities
- Clarify governance

**5. Partnering Maps** (`partnering-maps`)
- Identify key partners
- Assess partnership needs

### Step 3: Generate Business Model

```
## Business Model: [Business Name]

### Executive Summary
**Business**: [One-line description]
**Model Type**: [Subscription/Marketplace/SaaS/etc.]
**Target Market**: [Segment]
**Revenue Model**: [How money is made]
**Key Differentiator**: [What makes us unique]
```

## Notes

- Start with customer value, not product features
- Be specific about revenue model and pricing
- Validate assumptions with market research
- Consider scalability from the start
- Plan for capital needs early
