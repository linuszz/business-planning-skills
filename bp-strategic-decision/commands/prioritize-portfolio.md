# /prioritize-portfolio -- Portfolio Prioritization

Analyze and prioritize business portfolio for resource allocation.

## Invocation

```
/prioritize-portfolio Our 5 business units
/prioritize-portfolio Product portfolio for resource allocation
/prioritize-portfolio [portfolio description]
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Inventory Portfolio Items

List all portfolio elements:
- Business units
- Products/services
- Markets/segments
- Investment opportunities

### Step 2: Apply Portfolio Frameworks

**1. Growth-Share Matrix** (`growth-share-matrix`)
- Classify as Stars, Cash Cows, Question Marks, Dogs
- Identify portfolio balance

**2. Portfolio Matrix** (`portfolio-matrix`)
- Map on multiple dimensions
- Assess strategic fit and performance

**3. Prioritization Funnel** (`prioritization-funnel`)
- Screen and rank opportunities
- Apply criteria systematically

**4. Risk-Return Analysis**
- Balance risk across portfolio
- Diversification benefits

## Notes

- Balance is key — don't over-index on any one category
- Consider interdependencies between portfolio items
- Update portfolio view regularly (quarterly minimum)
- Be prepared to make hard choices on underperformers
- Factor in strategic direction, not just current performance
